This document provides testing guidelines for the novel coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19, formerly known as 2019-nCoV) associated with an outbreak of respiratory illness that originated in Wuhan, Hubei Province, China in late 2019. The causative agent for COVID-19 disease is SARS-CoV-2 virus. For the purpose of clear communication, PHO uses the term COVID-19 to refer to both the virus and the disease. PHO is not currently recommending routine testing of asymptomatic persons for COVID-19.
